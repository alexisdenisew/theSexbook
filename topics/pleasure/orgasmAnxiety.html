Orgasm Anxiety

Orgasm Anxiety

Orgasm anxiety can prevent becoming fully aroused or reaching climax. It can be incredibly frustrating, but it’s important to remember that 1) is not your fault and 2) it can be dealt with!

What’s orgasm anxiety / sexual performance anxiety?

Anxiety makes it more difficult to reach a climax. Your state of mind can have a huge impact on your ability to feel pleasure and orgasm.

Overcoming Orgasm Anxiety

  • ✿ There is no “cure-all” for orgasm anxiety, but feeling comfortable with the person you’re with and good communication is key. Be open with your partner.


  • ✿ Talk to friends or a therapist for advice


  • ✿ Remember, you don’t need to orgasm to experience pleasure and orgasming isn’t the only way to enjoy sexual activity


  • ✿ Practice self-love and body positivity


  • ✿ Foreplay is important! Warm yourself/your partner up first, it will help with arousal and general comfort
